About The Voices

The Voices is an emerging duology revolving around an imaginary world created by two girls with a love for writing and a vision.

This world started from a simple talk about their hobbies until it turned into a whole new literary adventure between the two.

The Voices is a story created by Micah Cruz, it follows the antics of two people who were once childhood friends, separated by a series of unfortunate events, on a journey to remember each other and their past.

Their First Symphony

Their First Symphony begins with high school senior, Jenna McCartney stepping into campus for the first time after spending most of her life homeschooled. She was immediately adopted into a new group on her first week. Everyone welcomed her warmly. Though it wasn't always easy for her to adapt to a new setting.

Grayson Sanders was one of the people who welcomed their new fellow senior. It wasn't difficult befriending her, mostly because the others had done all the work and you just don't have to view her frequent silence as a threat.

One thing they couldn't fathom though, is the sense of familiarity they have with each other in every interaction. They did learn that they've originated from the same world, but it seems like there's more. It's as though they've always known each other.

Finding Harmony

Four years after the events of The First Symphony and the passing of Jenna's father. Finding Harmony revolves around Grayson and Jenna as college seniors.

There have been strange events happening in Jenna's hometown the past years, and it's led up to the murder of Jenna's father. Filled with vengeance, Jenna's spent this whole time figuring out how to get back at the villains responsible for it. Grayson, out of his growing love for her, decides to help her in her endeavors. Upon doing so, they start to get glimpses of their past memories learning more of who they were before they had met in high school.

The Locations

Symphonièr

Symphonièr is a world made out of music and everyone is made of it and surrounded by it. We bearers have an ability to use the soul music, the indwelling magic that springs from the power of music, to do different things. This is where Jenna and Grayson came from.



The Mundane Realm

The Mundane Realm is the world we dwell in. "Earth" would've been the most familiar term. This is where Grayson and Jenna reside for half of the story.
